Brief Introduction About Myself
Akinbo Eniola Regina is a passionate creative and tech enthusiast with a background in Theatre and Media Arts from Ekiti State University. 
She earned a prestigious scholarship to study Content and Media Creation at Dominican University, where she developed a keen interest in filmmaking as a tool for storytelling and advocacy.
Committed to using media and technology for social impact, Eniola has expanded her expertise into the tech industry through the ALX pathway and currently contributes her skills at Hivenify. She thrives on collaboration, innovation, and community engagement values that align seamlessly with nonprofit initiatives.
Beyond her professional journey, Eniola is an avid reader, enjoys meaningful connections, and finds inspiration in films that provoke thought and change. She is driven by the power of storytelling and technology to amplify voices and create transformative experiences for communities.
